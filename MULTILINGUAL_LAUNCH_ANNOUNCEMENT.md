## Usage Tips

### LinkedIn:
- Post during business hours (9 AM - 12 PM IST)
- Use 3-5 relevant hashtags
- Tag relevant people/companies if appropriate
- Engage with comments within first hour
- Share in relevant groups

### WhatsApp:
- Personal messages: Send to close contacts first
- Status: Post 2-3 times over 24 hours
- Groups: Share once, don't spam
- Include direct link for easy access

### Timing:
- LinkedIn: Tuesday-Thursday, 9-11 AM
- WhatsApp Status: Evening (6-9 PM)
- Instagram/Facebook: Evening (7-9 PM)
- Twitter: Multiple times throughout day

### Engagement:
- Respond to all comments within 24 hours
- Ask questions to encourage discussion
- Share user feedback and testimonials
- Create follow-up posts with usage stats
