## ğŸ’¡ **Pro Tips**

1. **Start with your biggest supporters** (family, close friends)
2. **Make it about them** - "thought your network might find this useful"
3. **Provide multiple options** so they can choose what fits their style
4. **Be specific about what you need** - don't just say "help me promote"
5. **Show appreciation** - people remember how you treat them
6. **Keep it simple** - the easier you make it, the more likely they'll help
