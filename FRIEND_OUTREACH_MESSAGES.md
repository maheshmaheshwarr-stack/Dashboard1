## üìã **Quick Reference Checklist**

### **Before Reaching Out:**
- [ ] Personalize each message with their name
- [ ] Mention something specific about them/your relationship
- [ ] Have the share kit ready to send immediately
- [ ] Choose the right platform (WhatsApp for close friends, email for professional)

### **When Messaging:**
- [ ] Be clear about what you're asking for
- [ ] Emphasize that you have ready-made content
- [ ] Give them an easy way to say no
- [ ] Express genuine appreciation

### **After They Share:**
- [ ] Thank them promptly
- [ ] Acknowledge their specific post if you see it
- [ ] Offer to reciprocate in the future
- [ ] Keep them updated on your progress

---

## üí° **Pro Tips**

1. **Start with your biggest supporters** - family and closest friends first
2. **Be specific** - "Would you share this on Facebook?" vs "Help me promote this"
3. **Make it easy** - Always offer ready-made content
4. **Show appreciation** - Thank them before and after
5. **Don't be pushy** - Give them an easy out
6. **Follow up appropriately** - Thank them if they share, don't nag if they don't

---

**Remember:** Most people want to help friends succeed, but they need to know exactly what you need and you need to make it as easy as possible for them!
