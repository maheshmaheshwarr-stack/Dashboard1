Curation principles

The feed is filtered with an emphasis on positivity and usefulness, without ignoring reality. The intent is not to deny difficult news, but to make it easier to notice constructive and solution-oriented developments.

  • Highlight stories that teach something, show clear progress or unlock a new way of thinking.
  • Avoid sensationalist framing, clickbait and purely fear-driven narratives whenever possible.
  • Prioritise credibility of the source and clarity of the information.
