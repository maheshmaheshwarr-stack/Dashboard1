## About News Content

The **UI is fully translated** for all languages, but the **news content** itself is still mostly in English because:

1. **RSS Feed Availability:** Most news RSS feeds are in English
2. **International Sources:** BBC, Reuters, TechCrunch provide English content
3. **Regional Feeds:** Limited availability of reliable regional language RSS feeds

This is normal for multilingual news apps - the interface translates, but content depends on feed availability.
